Food and Nutrition

Foods dogs can eat

  • Boiled rice
  • Cooked chicken
  • Carrots
  • Apples without seeds

Foods dogs should not eat

  • Chocolate
  • Onions
  • Grapes
  • Spicy food
Food Item Safe for Dogs
Chicken Yes
Chocolate No

Daily Care

Walking: Dogs should be taken for a walk at least two times a day.

Grooming: Brushing helps remove loose hair and keeps the coat clean.

Bathing: Dogs should be bathed once in three to four weeks.

Health Basics

Vaccinations

  • Rabies
  • Distemper
  • Parvovirus

Common signs of illness

  • They act weird
  • Loss of appetite
  • Vomiting
  • Less energy than usual
  • Continuous scratching
